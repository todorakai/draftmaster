## Voiceover Notes

### Tone & Style:
- **Confident but not arrogant**
- **Enthusiastic but professional**
- **Clear and articulate**
- **Pacing**: Moderate to fast (match the energy of LoL)

### Voice Characteristics:
- **Gender**: Male or Female (both work)
- **Age**: 25-35 (relatable to target audience)
- **Accent**: Neutral English (American or British)
- **Energy**: 7/10 (excited but controlled)

- Record in quiet environment
